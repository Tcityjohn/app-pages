# TAP Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

## Overview

TAP is a mobile application that helps families negotiate screen time agreements together. This privacy policy explains how our app handles data and respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

We collect the following data to provide the app's functionality:

### Account Information

- Email address (for authentication)
- First name and family role (parent or teen)
- This information is required to create your account and manage family groups

### Camera & Photo Library

- The app uses your camera to take photos as proof of completed check-in tasks
- You can also select existing photos from your library
- Photos are uploaded and stored securely on our servers
- Photos are only visible to members of your family group

### Push Notifications

- We collect your device push token to send check-in reminders and family activity updates
- You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time

## Analytics (PostHog)

We use PostHog to understand how users interact with the app:

- We collect anonymous usage data (screens visited, features used)
- We do not collect personal information through analytics
- Analytics help us improve the app experience
- You can opt out of analytics in your device settings

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure:

- Account data (email, name, role) is stored in our database
- Check-in photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage
- Pact agreements and completion data are stored in our database
- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Collected |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| PostHog | Analytics | Anonymous usage events |
| Supabase | Backend & authentication | Account data, check-in data, photos |
| Expo Push Notifications | Reminders | Device push token |

## User Controls

- You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings in the app
- You can disable camera access in iOS Settings
- You can disable photo library access in iOS Settings
- You can disable push notifications in iOS Settings

## Children's Privacy

TAP is designed for use by families, including teens ages 9-17. Teen accounts are created with parental involvement through family invite codes. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the app's functionality. We do not serve ads or sell data.

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the App Store or visit our [Support](./support) page.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.

## Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with Apple's App Store guidelines and applicable privacy regulations.
