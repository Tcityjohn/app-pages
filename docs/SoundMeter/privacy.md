# Sound Levels Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-01-15

## Overview

Sound Levels (dB Meter) is a mobile application designed to help users measure and understand sound levels. This privacy policy explains how our app handles data and respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any audio data.**
- All sound measurements are processed in real-time on your device
- No audio recordings are saved or sent to our servers
- Your microphone is used only for instantaneous sound level measurements

## Microphone Usage

- The app uses your device's microphone to measure sound levels in real-time
- Measurements are temporary and not persistently stored
- Sound level data is immediately calculated and displayed, then discarded

## Third-Party Services

### Advertising (AdMob)
- We use Google AdMob for advertising
- AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

### In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)
- We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchase transactions
- Only transaction data necessary for purchase processing is collected
- No personal financial information is stored within the app

## User Controls

- You can stop sound measurements at any time
- You can upgrade to remove ads
- The app does not require or store any personal identification information

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the App Store.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Please review it periodically.

## Compliance

This policy is designed to be transparent about our data handling practices and to protect user privacy.
