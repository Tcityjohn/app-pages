# Hey Kitchen Support

Need help with Hey Kitchen? Find answers below or contact us.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Voice Commands

**Q: The app isn't hearing my voice commands correctly.**

A: Make sure you're in a quiet environment and speaking clearly. Tap the microphone button and wait for the listening indicator before speaking. Try phrases like:
- "Convert 2 cups of flour to grams"
- "Double this recipe"
- "Set a timer for 10 minutes"

**Q: What voice commands does Hey Kitchen understand?**

A: Hey Kitchen understands:
- **Unit conversions:** "How many grams in 1 cup of flour?"
- **Recipe scaling:** "Double this recipe" or "Scale for 8 servings"
- **Temperature:** "Convert 350 fahrenheit to celsius"
- **Timers:** "Set a timer for 20 minutes" (Plus feature)

### Recipe Scaling

**Q: How do I scale a recipe?**

A: Go to the Recipe Scaler tab, paste or type your ingredient list, then tap Double, Triple, or Half. You can also use voice commands like "double this recipe."

**Q: Can I scale to a specific number of servings?**

A: Yes! Enter your ingredients, set the original servings, then enter your target servings.

### Subscriptions

**Q: How do I restore my purchase?**

A: Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**

A: Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Find Hey Kitchen and tap Cancel
